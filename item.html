General Features of the Mini Spinning Machine

These are some of the characteristics that makes our machine outstanding and unique.

  • Usually, spinning machines need at least 7-13 meters high sheds. However, since this machine has a small size, it can be placed in any place without having to invest large capitals in special sheds.
  • Two Mini Spinning Machine could produce the same quantity produced by a conventional spinning machine.
  • Two Mini Spinning Machine have same price of a single conventional spinning machine.
  • Nevertheless, it benefits because it gives:
    • - Possibility of producing of two types of yarn simultaneously, colour and/or count.
    • - Possibility of serving two customers simultaneously.
    • - Possibility of continuing production with one machine while the other under maintenance.
    • - Possibility of fulfilling small orders.
  • The Mini Spinning Machine has 5 Godets which means 5 drawing steps and a better yarn quality.
  • The power consumption in small machines is much lower than in conventional machines. This means savings in production costs. (Mini Spinning Machine takes 20 kW installed and consumes only 13 kW during operation).
  • Mini Spinning Machine is easy to handle, this means greater ease of learning for workers.
  • On the other hand, customers no longer place orders for 1000 kg per colour; but they ask for 150-200 kg. Therefore, the Mini Spinning Machine is ideal for small orders.
  • The waste percentage produced from colour change by the Mini Spinning Machine is smaller in comparison to the conventional machines;
  • Expanding the yarn industry with our Mini Spinning Machine means varying production and more flexibility in serving customers. The traditional machine largely produces for a single specific item. Splitting the production with more Mini Spinning Machine means diversifying production.

The Mini Spinning Machine brings countless benefits, but for sure, it significantly cuts down costs when it comes to production.
